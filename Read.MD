# TewahdoHouse: Ethiopian & Eritrean Orthodox Community Library (BETA)

A modern, responsive web application dedicated to preserving and sharing the liturgical, theological, and historical treasures of the **Ethiopian Orthodox Tewahedo Church (EOTC)** and the **Eritrean Orthodox Tewahedo Church (ErOTC)**.

## 🌟 Vision
This project aims to be a digital repository for sacred resources, making them accessible to the global Orthodox community. We are currently in **Beta** and actively seeking contributions from community members.

## 🚀 Features
- **Unified Heritage:** Dedicated sections for both Ethiopian and Eritrean traditions, honoring their shared Aksumite history and liturgical unity.
- **Resource Hub:** Categorized library for Holy Qidase (Liturgy), Mezmur, Dogma, History, and Sunday School materials.
- **VIP Upload System:** A dedicated portal for community members to contribute their own Qidase PowerPoints, audios, and documents.
- **Responsive Design:** Optimized for mobile, tablet, and desktop viewing with a culturally respectful aesthetic.
- **Authentic Sources:** Direct links to official church archives and verified external resources.

## 📁 Sections
- **Holy Qidase (Liturgy):** Divine Liturgy PowerPoints and anaphoras.
- **Mezmur & Mahlet:** Sacred hymns and Yaredic chants.
- **Dogma & Theology:** The Five Pillars of Mystery and church teachings.
- **Hagiography & History:** Lives of the Saints (Senkessar) and Church milestones.
- **Sunday School:** Resources for youth and adult education.

## 🛠️ Built With
- **React** + **Vite**
- **Tailwind CSS**
- **Framer Motion** (for smooth animations)
- **Lucide React** (for icons)

## 🤝 How to Contribute
1. Join the **VIP Portal** on the website.
2. Use the **"Upload & Join VIP"** tab to share your first Qidase PowerPoint or Audio file.
3. Once verified, you will be recognized as a VIP contributor to the archive.
